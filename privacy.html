Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 19, 2024

Overview

Crystal Pop Journey ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Crystal Pop Journey collects minimal information to provide you with the best gaming experience:

Game Data (Stored Locally)

  • High scores and level progress
  • Game settings and preferences
  • Power-up inventory

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Game Center Integration

If you choose to use Game Center features:

  • Your Game Center ID and username may be used for leaderboards
  • Achievement data may be synced to Game Center
  • This data is managed by Apple's Game Center service

AdMob Advertising (Optional)

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. You can completely disable ads for FREE in the Settings menu.

When ads are enabled, Google AdMob may collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Approximate location (country/region)
  • Ad interaction data

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Donations

Crystal Pop Journey offers optional donations to support DMD (Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy) research and treatment. All donation transactions are processed securely by Apple's In-App Purchase system. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Children's Privacy

Crystal Pop Journey is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app stores game progress locally on the device only.

Data Security

Your game data is stored securely on your device using iOS standard storage mechanisms. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple Game Center - For leaderboards and achievements (Apple Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdMob - For advertising (optional, can be disabled) (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Apple In-App Purchases - For donation processing (Apple Privacy Policy)

Your Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Disable Ads: Turn off ads completely for FREE in the Settings menu
  • Reset Progress: Delete all local game data using the "Reset Progress" button in Settings
  • Game Center: Manage Game Center privacy in your iOS Settings

Data Retention

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not retain any data on external servers. Your data remains on your device until you:

  • Reset progress using the in-app button
  • Delete the app from your device

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@crystalpopjourney.com

Consent

By using Crystal Pop Journey, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
